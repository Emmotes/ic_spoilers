# Minthara

A member of the ancient House Baenre, Minthara is a True Soul in the service of the Absolute and one of the three leaders of the Goblin Camp in the Wilderness. She and her fellow True Souls, Priestess Gut and Dror Ragzlin, set up a base of operations in an old Sel√ªnite temple in search of a Mysterious Artefact by order of the Absolute.

[Minthara - BG3 Wiki](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Minthara)

# Basic Information

Minthara will be a new champion in the Feast of the Moon event on 6 November 2024.
