# Dragondown Augments

The augments for the upcoming Dragondown event.

> Every tier two or higher event variant completed automatically applies 100 Tiny Blacksmithing Contracts to the variant's Champion's equipment for free!  
</div></div>

</div></div>

</div></div>

</div></div>
